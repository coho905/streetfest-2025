Phi Delta Alpha’s annual summer fundraiser

Joing us July 19th to support the Upper Valley Haven!

Enjoy a full day of interactive activities sponsored by Dartmouth’s Greek organizations — all raising funds for our cause.

All funds raised benefit Upper Valley Haven — a local nonprofit providing food, shelter, and resources for individuals and families overcoming poverty.

Friends, families, and the greater Hanover community are warmly invited to attend and make a difference together.

Created during the Class of 2025’s sophomore summer, StreetFest celebrates Dartmouth’s sophomore performance groups while giving back to the community. Each year, proceeds support a new charitable cause chosen by the house.
